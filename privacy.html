個人情報の収集について Collection of Personal Information

本アプリは、氏名・メールアドレス・位置情報などの個人を特定できる情報を一切収集しません。すべてのデータはお使いのデバイス内にのみ保存され、外部サーバーへのデータ送信は行いません。

The App does not collect any personally identifiable information, including but not limited to your name, email address, or location data. All data you enter is stored solely on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

All purchase data (e.g., purchase history, transaction IDs) is managed by Apple's systems. For more information, please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy:

All data recorded within the App, including crop information and work logs, is stored locally on your device only. No data is transmitted to or stored on external servers.

データはアプリのストレージに保持されるため、アプリを削除した場合はすべてのデータが消去されます。事前にバックアップをお取りください。

Please note that uninstalling the App will permanently delete all stored data. We recommend backing up your data before uninstalling.

The App does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services. The App uses Apple's StoreKit framework solely for processing in-app purchases.
